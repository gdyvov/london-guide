Tate Modern

Bankside · Free collection · Former power station

International modern and contemporary art across the Boiler House, Blavatnik Building, and the vast Turbine Hall for large commissions. Permanent collection displays are free; special exhibitions are usually ticketed — book online for busy periods.

Getting there

Southwark Tube, Blackfriars, or a Thames-side walk from the Southbank Centre. The Millennium Bridge links St Paul’s to the main entrance — great for photos, busy on weekends.

Turbine Hall

The yearly commission fills this industrial space — worth checking what’s installed before you visit; some works are walk-through and family-friendly.
