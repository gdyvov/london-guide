Sky Garden

20 Fenchurch Street (“Walkie-Talkie”) · Free entry by advance booking

Landscaped terraces and a 360° view over the City and the Thames. Entry is free but you must reserve a time slot on the official Sky Garden website — walk-ups are not guaranteed. Sunset slots disappear fast; weekday lunch slots are often easier.

What to expect

Indoor gardens, viewing decks, and a café/bar (paid). Dress for wind on the outer terraces. Allow time for airport-style security at busy times.

Combine with

You’re a short walk from the Tower of London side of the river or Leadenhall Market — good for pairing a view with a cheap eat nearby.
