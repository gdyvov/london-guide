Museums & views

Major collections for free, plus one skyline spot that needs a free booking.

No ticket, still world-class

National museums are free to visit; special exhibitions may charge. Check hours before you go — weekday mornings are usually calmest.

Places to bookmark

British Museum

Rosetta Stone, Parthenon sculptures, and global collections. Free entry; weekday mornings are quieter at the door.

Bloomsbury

National Gallery

Van Gogh, Titian, Turner — by Trafalgar Square. Permanent galleries are free; audio guides and blockbusters are often paid.

Westminster

Sky Garden

Greenery and a wide view from the “Walkie-Talkie.” Free timed slots — book on the official site. Sunset slots feel special.

City

Tate Modern

Modern and contemporary art in a former power station on Bankside — free collection displays and a huge Turbine Hall.

Southwark

Natural History Museum

Dinosaurs, Hintze Hall’s blue whale, and Earth galleries — free entry (donations welcome). Quieter on weekday mornings.
