Coffee & breakfast

Drink-plus-pastry beats full brunch · Watch for “extras” on the till

Morning queues of locals are often a good sign for value. Filter or americano plus a croissant stays under most brunch bills; oat milk surcharges add up if you’re not careful.

Speciality cafés

Roasters may charge more but refills are rare — compare sizes (8oz vs 12oz) before you pay. Parks and gallery cafés can be pricey — grab takeaway and sit on steps or benches.

Supermarkets

Meal-deal coffee plus pastry from a chain supermarket is dull but honest on price when you’re between sights.
