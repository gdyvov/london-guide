National Gallery

Trafalgar Square · Free permanent collection

European paintings from the 13th to early 20th century: Italian Renaissance, Dutch masters, British portraits, and Impressionists. Entry to the main galleries is free; some temporary exhibitions and audio guides are paid.

Planning a visit

The Sainsbury Wing and main portico face Trafalgar Square. Weekends are busiest; quieter slots are often weekday mornings. Allow time for security.

Highlights

Van Gogh’s Sunflowers, Turner’s seascapes, and Holbein’s The Ambassadors draw steady crowds — use the room maps to route around them if you prefer a calmer path.
