Natural History Museum

Cromwell Road, South Kensington · Free entry (donations welcome)

From dinosaurs and the blue whale in Hintze Hall to volcanoes and gemstones, the museum is a full-day visit. Entry is free; some temporary exhibitions are paid and should be booked online in school holidays.

Queues

Weekends and school breaks are packed — arrive for opening or book a paid exhibition slot if you want a timed entry. South Kensington Tube is closest; the V&A and Science Museum are next door if you want to hop between free museums.

Hintze Hall

Look up at the blue whale skeleton and the Romanesque arches — it’s the usual “wow” moment on entry. Dippy the diplodocus tours nationally sometimes; check what’s on display before you go.
